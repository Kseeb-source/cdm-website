Who we are

BORN ON THE STREET

Dough One started with a wood-fired oven, a beat-up trailer, and an obsession with getting the perfect leopard-charred crust. Every base is slow-fermented for 48 hours from our own sourdough starter, and every topping is sourced from the best local and Italian suppliers we could find.

We pitch up at markets, festivals, and private events across the region — bringing proper Neapolitan pizza to wherever the people are.
