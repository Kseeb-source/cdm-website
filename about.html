About the company

Systems built for
the businesses
who run them.

Carpe DM Strategies Ltd is a London-based digital services company. We build the websites, automation and growth systems that trades and service businesses actually need to run — and we run our own business on the exact same stack.

Our story

Built from experience,
not just theory.

Carpe DM Strategies started because we watched too many good businesses lose work online. Not because the work was not good enough — but because they could not afford the tools the big firms use to capture, manage and grow their enquiries.

So we built those tools ourselves. We used them on our own sister company Damp & Mould Solutions UK, a specialist property-care business operating across London. We proved the stack worked end-to-end, from the first Google click to the final invoice.

Now we offer that same stack to other trades and service businesses. A fast, professional website. A CRM that handles quotes, jobs and invoices. Enquiry capture, lead qualification and follow-up on autopilot. Google Ads, SEO and content that compounds every month.

One company, one stack, one point of accountability. Built here in London, for businesses here in the UK.

Platform purpose

What this platform does.

The Carpe DM Strategies platform at www.carpedmstrategies.co.uk is an owned-and-operated business website. Here is exactly what it is used for, and how it fits together with the services we deliver.

01 — Public site

Market our own services

This website describes the services we offer, showcases the systems we build, and explains how we work. Visitors can read about our approach, browse FAQs, and get in touch.

02 — Lead capture

Receive and qualify enquiries

Prospective clients submit enquiries through the contact form. Each enquiry is routed into our internal CRM where we qualify it, prepare a proposal and book a strategy call.

03 — Advertising

Google Ads & Google Ads API

We run Google Ads campaigns to reach UK businesses that may benefit from our services. We use the Google Ads API only to manage, report on and optimise our own advertising accounts and the advertising accounts of our direct clients — never resold, never exposed to third parties.
