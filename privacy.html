Who We Are

Carpe DM Strategies Ltd ("Carpe DM Strategies", "we", "us", "our") is a London-based digital services company that builds websites, automation, CRM and growth systems for trades and service businesses in the United Kingdom.

This Privacy Policy sets out how we collect, use and protect personal data that is provided to us through this website (www.carpedmstrategies.co.uk) and through the services we deliver to our clients.

Scope

This policy applies to:

  • Visitors to this website
  • Prospective clients who contact us through the enquiry form, email or phone
  • Clients of Carpe DM Strategies Ltd
  • Third parties whose personal data we process while delivering services to our clients (for example, enquiries submitted through a client's website that we host or automate)

What Information We Collect

Information you give us

  • Full name
  • Business or company name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Details of your business and what you are looking for
  • Any other information you choose to share in correspondence with us

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, browser and operating system
  • Pages visited, referring URL and time on page
  • Cookie identifiers set by analytics and advertising services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and arrange a strategy call
  • Prepare and send quotations, contracts and project documentation
  • Deliver the services you have engaged us to provide
  • Issue invoices and process payments
  • Send service-related updates and administrative messages
  • Measure and improve the performance of our website and advertising
  • Comply with our legal, tax and accounting obligations

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Advertising, Analytics & the Google Ads API

We use Google Analytics and Google Search Console to measure how visitors use this website, which pages perform best and where our traffic comes from. These services set first-party cookies and process aggregated usage data on our behalf.

We also run paid advertising through Google Ads to reach businesses in the United Kingdom that may benefit from our services.

Where we use the Google Ads API, it is used exclusively by Carpe DM Strategies Ltd to:

  • Manage, report on and optimise our own Google Ads accounts
  • Manage, report on and optimise the Google Ads accounts of direct clients of Carpe DM Strategies Ltd, under an existing service agreement and with each client's explicit authorisation
  • Build internal dashboards and reporting that help us deliver better advertising outcomes to our clients

We do not resell access to the Google Ads API, we do not expose the API to unauthenticated third parties, and we do not use data obtained through the API for any purpose other than managing the advertising accounts it was obtained from.

Cookies

This website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep the site working correctly (essential cookies)
  • Measure how the site is used (Google Analytics)
  • Measure the performance of our advertising (Google Ads conversion tracking)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to browse this site.

Legal Bases for Processing

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — to deliver the services you have engaged us for, or to take steps at your request before entering into a contract
  • Legitimate interests — to respond to enquiries, measure the performance of our website and advertising, and secure our systems
  • Consent — for non-essential cookies and any marketing communications you have specifically opted into
  • Legal obligation — where we are required by law to retain certain records (for example, tax and company records)

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out above, including:

  • Enquiry information: up to 24 months from last contact, unless a service agreement is entered into
  • Client records and service documentation: the duration of the engagement plus 7 years, in line with UK statutory record-keeping requirements
  • Website analytics: up to 26 months

Who We Share Data With

We only share personal data with trusted processors who help us run our business, including:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Email and communications providers
  • Payment processors
  • Accounting and bookkeeping services
  • Analytics and advertising platforms (Google Analytics, Google Ads)

All processors are contractually bound to handle data in line with UK data protection law.

International Transfers

Some of our processors are located outside the United Kingdom. Where this is the case, we rely on UK-approved transfer mechanisms (such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement or adequacy regulations) to ensure your data remains protected.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data, where applicable
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss or alteration. Access to personal data is limited to the people who need it to perform their role.

Changes to This Policy
